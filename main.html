Kodaikanal, India


Kodaikanal, often referred to as the "Princess of Hill Stations," is a beautiful hill town located in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. Known for its serene environment, lush greenery, and cool climate, Kodaikanal is a perfect getaway for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful retreat.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. odaikanal Lake: A star-shaped man-made lake, ideal for boating and picnics.
  2. Coaker's Walk: A scenic pedestrian path offering stunning views of the valleys.
  3. Berjam Lake : A tranquil reservoir surrounded by a dense forest.
  4. Pillar Rocks: Majestic rock formations providing a breathtaking view.
  5. Silver Cascade Falls:A spectacular 180-feet high waterfall.

Must-Try Foods

  • Homemade Chocolats : Kodaikanal is famous for its variety of delicious homemade chocolates.
  • Hot Tea : Enjoy a cup of freshly brewed tea made from locally grown tea leaves.
  • Herbal Soup :A healthy and flavorful soup made from local herbs.
  • Fruit Preserves : Try the locally made fruit jams and preserves.
  • Spicy Chettinad Cusine : Relish the traditional spicy dishes from the Chettinad region.
